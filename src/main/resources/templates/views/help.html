Welcome to the helping section!

The following section is designed to help you workaround with the application. Please select one of the following topics to find out more about:

  1. Navigate through pages
  2. Create logs
  3. View logs
  4. Edit logs
  5. Mark log as completed
  6. Delete log
  1. Navigate through pages
    • Use the navigation bar drawer button to walk around pages.
    • To return to the home page you can either click the 'Home' link from the drawer navigation or click the ducky logo.
    • Use the floating action buttons to operate content.
    • Use the buttons on each log to do modifications on the whole content.
    • Click the 'View' button on any log to view its content
  2. Create logs
    • Click the floating action button in the home page.
    • Fill in the form.
    • When you're done click save.
    • Don't forget to name your log!
  3. View logs
    • On the home page click the 'View' button of the related log card.
  4. Edit Logs
    • On the home page click the 'View' button of the related log card.
    • Inside the log click the pencil-shaped icon and an Edit form will open.
    • After you're done click save.
  5. Mark log as completed
    • On the matching log card, click the checkbox
  6. Delete log
    • On the matching log card, click the trash icon.
    • Confirm your deletion.
